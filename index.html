About Me

Greetings! I'm a Colorado native with an insatiable curiosity and a deep-rooted passion for learning and discovery. My academic journey led me to a Bachelor's Degree in Psychology from the University of Colorado, Boulder, and it's there that I discovered my fascination with data and statistics.

Undergraduate Degree and Research Experience

During my undergraduate years, I was drawn to the realm of data and its role in shaping our understanding of human behavior. Courses in Statistics and Research Methods ignited my interest in data collection, analysis, experimental design, hypothesis testing, and statistical programming. This fascination propelled me to a Research Assistant role in the Emotion, Decision Making, Judgement, and Identity (EDJI) lab on campus, where I participated in a compelling project focused on political bias in helping behaviors. This endeavor culminated in the completion of an Honors Thesis, a comprehensive exploration that encompassed experiment design, data collection, data analysis, and a thesis defense presentation.

Entering the Field of Data Analytics

My path into the world of Data Analytics unfolded after I completed my tenure as a Whitewater Raft Guide and River Manager. I discovered the field of Data Analytics and the allure of harnessing data to drive informed decision-making for organizations instantly resonated with me. I embarked on a journey to bolster my background in statistics and experimental psychology with modern data analytics tools and skills. This process culminated in the achievement of the Google Data Analytics Certificate and the Google Advanced Data Analytics Certificate through Coursera. These programs deepened my knowledge of statistics, data, and analysis, while also honing my technical prowess, including relational database use and SQL querying, and enhancing my programming skills in Python and R. Now, equipped with a robust foundation in data analytics and research experience, I'm poised to embark on a Data Analytics career, blending my undergraduate background with technical skills from the Google Career Certificates.
